About Us

6007 began in 2013, at Millikan Science Acadedmy. We have been competing since Sack Attack and have competed at worlds four times, during Toss Up, Skyrise, Starstruck, and In the Zone. We are best known for winning the Science Division in 2017, and for winning the California State Championship twice. 6007 is also devoted to giving back to the community, providing mentorship to younger teams, and remaining available to answer teams’ questions.

8762 Alliance

The 8762 Alliance is an alliance that has been formed by some of the top teams in California, including the 8000 teams, team 62A, team 6007X, and the 7700 rolling robots teams. Every included team has won their division at worlds at least once, with the exception of 62, who was skills champion in 2015. The purpose of this is alliance is to share ideas and to work together to improve them.
