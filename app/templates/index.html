About Us

6007 was started by two interested middle schoolers at the Millikan Science Acadedmy. They knew practically nothing about vex, but with the help of some manuals and parents, the team enjoyed some moderate sucess in Sack Attack. Enboldened, they added four additional team members the following year and made it to the MS World Championships, getting eliminated in the quarterfinals. The next year, the team split into three, due to some administrative issues. The number 6007 was taken by one of the initial two students, Aeden Gasser-Brennan. Aeden, along with Ben Adelstein, a beginner who was brought onto the team that year, made one of the better middle school Skyrise robots. However, due to a freak accident at worlds, they went from first ranked to not making eliminations at worlds that year. During Nothing But Net, 6007's first High School year, Aeden went esentially solo, but was not able to do very well. Last year, in Starstruck, Aeden and Ben reunited and were able to rank 5th in the world in High School. This year, 6007 (now officially 6007X) hopes to replicate this sucess with our new members, Ethan and John, and as a Rolling Robots team.

8762 Alliance

The 8762 Alliance is an alliance that has been formed by some of the top teams in California, including the 8000 teams, team 62A, team 6007X, and the 7700 rolling robots teams. Every included team has won their division at worlds at least once, with the exception of 62, who was skills champion in 2015. The purpose of this is alliance is to share ideas and to work together to improve them.
